panelbar.js

Panelbar widget (like Mozilla sidebar).

Objects

OAT.Panelbar(fadeDelay)


Creates a panelbar. Its main div is accessible as panelbar.div . Fading speed can be controlled via fadeDelay .

Methods

OAT.Panelbar.addPanel(clicker, element)


Adds a new panel, contained in element . It will be shown after clicking on clicker .

OAT.Panelbar.go(index)


Switches to appropriate panel based on index .
